Earthbux is a transparent giving platform where your donation funds real missions, executed by vetted organizations, tracked publicly from day one to delivery.

What we are

A giving platform built for accountability.

Most donation platforms move money from your wallet to an organization and call that the end of the story. Earthbux treats that as the beginning. Every dollar donated is tied to a specific initiative and a specific elected organization, with funds released in weekly tranches only after that organization files verified progress reports.

Benefactors hold credit tokens that track the value of their contributions over time. If an organization underperforms, their credit value drops. If they deliver, it rises — and so does yours.

The EBX Token

Every dollar donated converts to EBX tokens. These tokens represent your stake in a mission. Their value is modulated by org transparency, community sentiment, and verified outcomes — not speculation.

How it works

From donation to delivery.

1
Causes rotate on a cycle
Seven causes — Atmosphere, Oceans, Forests, and more — take turns in the spotlight on a weekly cycle. The active cause receives the platform's full funding attention for its window.
2
Benefactors propose and vote on initiatives
Any member can propose a concrete initiative for the active cause. The community debates, rates, and votes. The top initiative enters the election phase where vetted organizations compete to execute it.
3
Organizations are elected and funded
Benefactors elect the organization they trust most to carry out the initiative. Once elected, funds are released weekly — but only after verified progress reports are filed and reviewed.
4
Progress is public, credit is earned
Every milestone, budget line, and fund release is visible to the whole platform. Benefactors earn credit tokens that reflect real-world mission outcomes, not promises.
The seven causes

Every cause. Every cycle.

Earthbux focuses on seven planetary cause areas. Funding rotates through all seven on a fixed cycle, ensuring every cause receives attention and no single issue monopolizes the platform.

Transparency

Every dollar, tracked.

Earthbux publishes budget tables, milestone timelines, fund release schedules, and progress reports for every active mission. There are no silent transfers. No "overhead" line that swallows your donation.

If an organization misses a milestone, funds are withheld until the issue is resolved. If they miss too many, the community can vote to reassign the mission to a different organization.

Organizations

Vetted, accountable orgs.

Only verified organizations can run missions on Earthbux. Vetting includes legal status checks, financial history review, and an editorial review of the org's track record. Verification is renewed each funding cycle.

Organizations earn a public score based on community reviews, on-time milestone completion, and budget accuracy. This score affects their standing in future elections.
