We need recipients for our donations! If you represent a charity organization — or want to suggest one — register here. Once an initiative wins its vote, registered orgs can submit a mission page and compete in the org election.

Register on your profile page with a name, brief description, and a point of contact.

Submit a mission page detailing your plan, budget, and measurable goals for the initiative you want to run.
03
Win the org election
Benefactors vote on which org runs the mission. One person, one vote.
04
Execute & report
Funds release in stages tied to milestones. Earthbux News tracks and publishes your progress.
