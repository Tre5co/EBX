How Earthbux works

01

Donate & receive EBX

Each cause runs a 7-week debate. Donate to receive earthbucks with a living credit that modulates your token\'s value as the mission progresses.

02

Debate & commit

Benefactors and orgs propose initiatives; benefactors commit EBX to the ones they support. The leader is decided at the end of the window.

03

Elect an organization

The org race runs alongside the debate. One person, one vote. The winning org gets 1/16 of the pool immediately; the rest unlocks over time.

04

Follow the mission

Track progress and spending in real time. When Earthbux judges the issue resolved, a buyback begins. Active missions live in the feed.
