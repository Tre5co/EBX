How Earthbux works

01

Donate & receive EBX

Every week a new pool forms from donations for the cause whose decision is coming up. You receive earthbucks with a living credit that modulates your token's value as the mission progresses.

02

Debate & commit

Each cause runs a 7-week debate. Benefactors and orgs propose initiatives; benefactors commit EBX to the ones they support. The leader is decided at the end of the window.

03

Elect an organization

The org race runs alongside the debate for the same 7 weeks. One person, one vote. The winning org receives 1/16 of the pool immediately; the rest unlocks over time, contingent on reporting.

04

Follow the mission

Track progress and spending in real time. When Earthbux judges the issue resolved, a buyback begins — returning value to benefactors in cash or other EBX. Active missions live in the feed.
