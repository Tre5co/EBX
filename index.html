How a mission works

Donors elect the missions and who runs them. Funds release in stages tied to milestones, and Earthbux News tracks every step.

01
Week 0 — Initiative decision
Benefactors propose initiatives and split their vote across them. The strongest initiative wins and becomes the week's mission.
02
Week 8 — Organization decision
Registered organizations compete to run the elected mission. One vote, one organization — the winner claims the pool.
03
Week 16 — Funds released
Committed funds release in stages as the mission hits its milestones, with EN publishing parallel progress reports.
