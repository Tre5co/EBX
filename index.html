How Earthbux works

01

Donate & receive EBX

Every two weeks a new pool forms from donations. You receive earthbucks with a living credit that modulates your token's value as the mission progresses.

02

Decide the next initiative

Benefactors and organizations propose ideas for each cause. Commit EBX to the ones you support. The leading initiative goes to an org vote.

03

Elect an organization

One person, one vote. The winning org gets 1/16 of the pool immediately. The rest unlocks over 12 weeks, contingent on their reporting.

04

Follow the mission

Track progress and spending in real time. When Earthbux judges the issue resolved, a buyback begins — returning value to benefactors in cash or other EBX.
