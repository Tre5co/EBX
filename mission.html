Any organization can run and receive votes without registering — a first vote nominates it. Leading organizations will be approved or rejected soon. Until an org is approved it's capped at 1 vote (10 EBX) per benefactor; if an org is rejected, every EBX committed to it is returned to its backers. An org must submit a mission statement to be elected.
Charity status & the pool guarantee

Earthbux works with organizations to apply for legal nonprofit status, making every donation go further. The application begins as soon as the election is won; donations cannot flow to the organization until nonprofit status is achieved. Earthbux helps with the whole process.

If no organization claims the mission it is not cancelled — advances still send, the community executes as best it can, and Earthbux News still reports. A nominated organization has until the start of Phase 4 to claim.

Organization Agreement

Required before an organization can take authority over a mission.

Representative attestation & terms (draft)

  1. I am an authorized representative of the organization named, and the information I have provided about it is truthful and accurate.
  2. I understand Earthbux News (EN) will verify this organization's identity during the budgeting phase, and that misrepresentation is grounds for removal, public disclosure, and legal action.
  3. I understand the initial advance is released at the start of the budgeting phase and that the full charity pool is unlocked only after verification and against agreed budget and reporting obligations.
  4. I agree to operate the mission in good faith — to publish a budget, report progress honestly, and direct funds to the stated cause and beneficiary.
  5. I acknowledge that accepting authority over the budget and mission sequence is a binding commitment, and that funds obtained through fraud may be recovered through litigation.
